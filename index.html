Cardio Monitoring

Cardio monitoring involves the continuous or periodic monitoring of a patient's heart activity to assess its function and detect any abnormalities. This process typically utilizes electrocardiography (ECG or EKG) to record the heart's electrical activity, providing valuable information about heart rate, rhythm, and potential cardiac issues.

Medical Treatment

Medical treatment refers to interventions aimed at alleviating symptoms, curing diseases, managing chronic conditions, or improving overall health and wellbeing. It encompasses a wide range of approaches, including medication, surgery, therapy, lifestyle modifications, and preventive measures.

Emergency Help

Emergency help refers to immediate assistance provided to individuals facing life-threatening situations or acute medical emergencies. This assistance can be provided by emergency medical services (EMS) personnel, including paramedics and emergency medical technicians (EMTs), as well as by trained bystanders or healthcare professionals.

First Aid

First aid is the initial assistance given to someone who has been injured or suddenly taken ill. It aims to preserve life, prevent the condition from worsening, and promote recovery. Basic first aid techniques include assessing the situation, ensuring safety for both the rescuer and the injured person.
